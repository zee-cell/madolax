WORKSHOP EXPERIENCE

Two of my STUDENTS (Sihle Masuku and Bhekumuzi Khumalo) have went on to be nominated for one of the biggest awards ceremonies in the country (Bulawayo Arts Awards) for their outstanding performances in the high school drama competitions. Furthermore I have found pleasure in working with INTWASA ARTS FESTIVAL in their arts projects for young emerging artists. They usually host theatre and poetry workshops and in these events I usually conduct daily exercises, vocal training, Dance, music and scene devising. I also help in hosting schools and community interruptive programs.
