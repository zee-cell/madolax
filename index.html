Don Franco

Tebokgo Foshius Makua

Tebokgo Foshius Makua popularly known as Don_Franco is a Hip Hop Artist from Diepsloot North of Johannesburg, and is one of the African Trap Star in the LA_Fam family of Artists.

He grew up in Kilbarchan Indian Village (Newcastle) where he fell in love with music at the age of 12 performing in Shows like {******************} Don_Franco matriculated in the year of 2012 in Kwazulu Nathal, before coming to Johannesburg to fulfill his ambitions and is keen to build a career in the music industry.
